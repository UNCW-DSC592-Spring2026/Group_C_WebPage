App Review Rating Prediction System

Using machine learning to analyze app reviews and predict rating categories

Synopsis

The App Review Rating Prediction System is a machine learning solution designed to analyze large volumes of free-text app reviews and predict rating outcomes in a structured format.

The system processes user-generated reviews and predicts ratings using a simplified star-based scale. Instead of using a continuous scale, reviews are categorized into three groups: High (4–5 stars), Medium (3 stars), and Low (1–2 stars). This classification makes the results easier to interpret and more useful for decision-making.

To improve prediction quality, the system gives more importance to longer and more informative reviews while reducing the impact of short or less meaningful ones. It is also designed to better handle complex language patterns such as sarcasm, misleading wording, and potentially unreliable or low-quality reviews.

Risk Register

The risk register identifies key risks that may affect the success of the project, including data, technical, and process-related risks.

Data-related risks include class imbalance, low-quality reviews, and difficulty detecting sarcasm. Technical risks include issues with model performance, reproducibility, and Azure ML resources. Process risks include delays and inconsistencies in team deliverables.

Each risk has been evaluated based on likelihood and impact, and mitigation strategies have been proposed to reduce potential negative effects.
